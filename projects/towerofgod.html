Tower of god

Tower of God is made for the Lumdum Dare 45, It's a god simulator. Minions try to build a tower to breach the sky, as god you're not happy about your creation and try to erridicate them with your mighty powers.

Lumdum Dare 45

Lumdum Dare is a game jam, you have 72h to make a game based on a theme that is given at the start of it. It’s quite popular, and is a good way to challenge yourself and meet other developpers.
