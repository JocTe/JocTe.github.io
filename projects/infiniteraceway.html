Infinite Raceway

Infinite Raceway is an endless scroller, inspired by the digital derby toy made in the 1979. It has a futuristic style in a top-down view in 2D. It was made in 3 weeks and was my first Unity project. It was a combination of all the skill that I learned during my first year at Isart Digitals.

Role
 Game Designer, Art and Code
For
 Yonders
Type
 2D Endless Scroller
Tools
 Unity, Photoshop, Word

What is it?

              This is the toy that inspired the project, my goal was to recreate it, for a webversion. Released in 1978 by Tommy, it display a scrolling tape divided into two roads. Each road had a car that you had to avoid, the player can interract with the wheel and the shift (3 speed) that give you the possibility to move your car and make the “scrolling tape” faster. The goal was to make the most lap under a time constraint, hitting cars will lower the score.
